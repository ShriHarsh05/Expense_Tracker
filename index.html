Privacy Policy for Smart Expense Tracker

Last updated: December 21, 2024

1. Introduction

Smart Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 SMS Messages

Our app requires access to your SMS messages to automatically detect expense transactions. We specifically look for:

  • Transaction notifications from banks and payment services
  • Expense-related keywords (debited, paid, purchased, etc.)
  • Amount information for expense tracking

2.2 User Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Unique user identifier

2.3 Expense Data

We store expense information including:

  • Transaction amounts
  • Expense categories
  • Transaction dates and times
  • Merchant information (when available)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 SMS Processing

  • SMS messages are processed locally on your device
  • Only expense-related information is extracted and stored
  • Full SMS content is never transmitted to our servers
  • SMS processing is used solely for expense detection

3.2 Machine Learning

  • We use AI to learn your categorization preferences
  • Learning patterns are stored to improve future categorization
  • All learning data is user-specific and not shared

3.3 Data Storage

  • Expense data is stored securely in Google Firebase
  • Each user's data is completely isolated
  • No cross-user data sharing occurs

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Firebase provides enterprise-grade security
  • User authentication through Google Sign-In
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase for secure data storage
  • Google Sign-In for authentication
  • When required by law or legal process

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored expense data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Revoke SMS permissions at any time
  • Export your expense data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Data Retention

  • Expense data is retained until you delete your account
  • Learning patterns are automatically cleaned up after 6 months of inactivity
  • Account deletion removes all associated data permanently

8. Permissions Explanation

8.1 SMS Permissions

  • READ_SMS: To scan existing messages for expense transactions
  • RECEIVE_SMS: To automatically detect new expense messages
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Required by SMS reading functionality

8.2 Internet Permission

  • Required for Firebase data synchronization
  • Google Sign-In authentication
  • App updates and security patches

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Geographic Restrictions

This app is primarily designed for users in India and supports Indian banking and payment systems. Users in other regions may have limited functionality.
